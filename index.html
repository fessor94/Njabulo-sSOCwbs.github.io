👨‍💻 About Me

Hey, I'm Njabulo — a naturally curious, tech-loving person currently diving into the world of cybersecurity. I have recently passed the ISC² CC (Certified in Cybersecurity). I've always been fascinated by how computers and networks work behind the scenes — whether it's figuring out how data travels or understanding the ins and outs of different systems, I'm hooked.

Cybersecurity — especially Security Operations Centers (SOC) — really speaks to me because I enjoy solving problems, spotting patterns, and staying one step ahead of potential threats. I come from a customer-facing background, so I'm used to staying calm under pressure, communicating clearly, and getting things done.

Right now, I'm self-teaching Python and digging into the technical side of cybersecurity: TCP/IP, firewalls, ACLs, network troubleshooting, and tools like Wireshark. I've been learning through HackTheBox, online labs, and a ton of YouTube deep dives. When it comes to tech, I'm also the go-to person in my family and friend group — if something stops working, I'm usually the one getting the call!
