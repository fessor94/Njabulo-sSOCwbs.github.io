👨‍💻 About Me

Hey, I’m Njabulo — a naturally curious, tech-loving person currently diving into the world of cybersecurity. I have recent passed the ISC2 CC(Ceritfied in Cybersecurity). I've always been fascinated by how computers and networks work behind the scenes. Whether it’s figuring out how data travels or understanding the ins and outs of different systems, I’m hooked. I also love gaming and, out of pure curiosity, I’ll Google just about anything tech-related that pops into my head.

Cybersecurity — especially Security Operations Centers (SOC) — really speaks to me because I enjoy solving problems, spotting patterns, and staying one step ahead of potential threats. I come from a customer-facing background, so I’m used to staying calm under pressure, communicating clearly, and getting things done. I’m excited to keep growing in this field and eventually work as part of a team defending systems and making a real impact.

Right now, I’m self-teaching Python and digging into the technical side of cybersecurity — things like TCP/IP, firewalls, ACLs, network troubleshooting, and using tools like Wireshark. I’ve been learning through HackTheBox, online labs, and a ton of YouTube deep dives. And honestly, I love every second of it. When it comes to tech, I’m also the go-to person in my family and friend group — if something stops working, I’m usually the one getting the call!
